About

Think of the Speed Hack as a “performance enhanced” hackathon. We think it’s a very effective, practical and fun way to promote APIs to developers.

There will be eight API providers you might recognize each having defined a tough, strictly time-limited coding challenge. Teams of three developers will compete to solve the challenges and increase their scores over three hours. The team with the most points wins. In case of equal points, we will have special tie breaking challenges.
